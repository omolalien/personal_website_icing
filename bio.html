Anisa's Biodata

As you know, my name is Nur Anisa Nazihah binti Kelana. I was born on 26th September 2004 and I am 20 years old. I was born in Hospital Putrajaya, Putrajaya. When I was 4 years old, I moved to and lived in Sabak Bernam, Selangor.

I pursued my early education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Doktor Abdul Latiff in Sabak Bernam. After completing my primary education, I attended SMK Ungku Aziz in Sabak Bernam, where I developed an interest in subjects such as Sports Science, Visual Arts Education, and Technical Communication Graphics. I really loved drawing when I was in school. As a child, I wanted to be a painter but realized it would be challenging to explore art in detail. Currently, I am pursuing a Diploma in Informatics Library at UiTM Kedah, which focuses on becoming a librarian. I hope to achieve good results this semester and graduate in the future.

My MBTI Personality is ENFJ, which stands for Extraverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging. People might think that I am quiet and introverted, but I am actually extroverted and enjoy talking and sharing things I like with others. Moreover, I spend my time reading novels, listening to music, watching movies, and taking photographs. I enjoy reading fiction novels, particularly in the genres of fantasy, manga, and mystery. I love taking pictures of cats, nature, and building architecture. I also enjoy visiting museums because they help me relax and explore new information and knowledge.

I hope you are all doing well and that this gives you more insight into who I am. Hope we meet again, and have a nice day.(˶ᵔ ᵕ ᵔ˶)
